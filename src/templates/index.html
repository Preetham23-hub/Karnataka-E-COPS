The Karnataka Police is the law enforcement agency for the state of Karnataka in India. The Karnataka Police has its headquarters in Bangalore, the state capital. The motto of the force is सेवार्थ कटिबद्धता, which means "committed to serve". Karnataka was formed by the merger of the erstwhile princely states.
